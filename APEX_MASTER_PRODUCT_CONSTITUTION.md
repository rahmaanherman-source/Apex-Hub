## Platform Rule

Every feature must connect to the rest of the platform.

Nothing is isolated.

Every page can communicate with every service through reusable APIs.

The platform should feel like walking into a single command center where every capability is available, connected, and discoverable from one place.

The goal is not to copy existing software.

The goal is to provide one cohesive hub that brings together customer management, commerce, AI assistance, operations, automation, analytics, media, governance, and administration in a unified experience.

When new functionality is added, it must integrate into this ecosystem instead of becoming a separate application.
